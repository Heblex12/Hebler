My name is Hebler Saucedo, I am originally from Mexico. My goal is to become a Frontend Web Developer, that is the reason why I enroll in this class at Bellevue College. Some of my hobbies are playing basketball, hiking, swimming at the gym, and playing video games. I get inspired a lot by nature and by things that surround me. The theme of this website was inspired by water and the Ocean that is why I chose these colors. I feel like I did the best I can with my current level of knowledge, but I hope to make it better as I learn more. I have learned a lot in this class, but one thing I want to get better at is Javascript, that is something I am still having trouble with. I will continue taking classes and in the future I hope to become a Full Stack Developer.
